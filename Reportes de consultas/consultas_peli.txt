Título: Castle in the Sky
Descripción: The orphan Sheeta inherited a mysterious crystal that links her to the mythical sky-kingdom of Laputa. With the help of resourceful Pazu and a rollicking band of sky pirates, she makes her way to the ruins of the once-great civilization. Sheeta and Pazu must outwit the evil Muska, who plans to use Laputa's science to make himself ruler of the world.
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Productor: Isao Takahata
Año de lanzamiento: 1986
Duración: 124 minutos
Calificación 95
Personajes:
 - Lusheeta Toel Ul Laputa
 - Pazu
 - Captain Dola
 - Colonel Muska
 - General Mouro
 - Uncle Pom
 - Laputian Robot
